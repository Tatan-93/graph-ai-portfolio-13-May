The Scientist

About Me

I am a dynamic Biologist specialized in medical biology and epidemiology. Currently a Scientific Collaborator at the University of Geneva's Institute of Global Health, my work focuses on the temporal and spatial dynamics of Yellow Fever.

Previously, I spent four years at the National Reference Center for Influenza (HUG), managing epidemiological studies and virus surveillance. My academic journey includes a Master’s in Medical Biology from UNIL and research in molecular biology.

This portfolio tracks my evolution into AI-augmented research, leveraging LLMs and automation tools to enhance scientific communication and data analysis.
